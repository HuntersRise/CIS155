Star Wars: Andor

Star Wars: Andor is a Disney+ series that explores the early days of the Rebellion against the Empire, focusing on Cassian Andor. The series gives fans a deeper look into the characters and events leading up to Rogue One: A Star Wars Story.

Why You Should Watch Andor:

  • In-depth character development
  • Exciting action sequences
  • Gripping storytelling
